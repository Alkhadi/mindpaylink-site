SOS — 60‑second Reset

A fast, guided cycle (default 4‑4‑6‑0: Inhale 4s • Hold 4s • Exhale 6s • Hold 0s) for one minute. Voice/haptics optional. Tap the orb or press Space to pause/resume.

Setup

Instructions

  1. Sit upright or lie flat; relax jaw and shoulders.
  2. Inhale through the nose; exhale through the mouth (softly).
  3. Follow the prompts. If dizzy, pause and reduce counts.

You can tweak the pattern from the menu above. Exhale‑longer patterns (e.g., 4‑4‑6‑0) often feel more calming.
