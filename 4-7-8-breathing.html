4-7-8 Breathing — Setup

Pattern: inhale 4 → hold 7 → exhale 8. (No final hold.)

Quick Session

Tip: Exhale should be long and gentle. Tap the orb or press Space to pause/resume.

Quick Session

Tip: Exhale should be long and gentle. Tap the orb or press Space to pause/resume.

How to Practice

  1. Comfortable posture; soften shoulders and jaw.
  2. Inhale through the nose; exhale smoothly through the mouth.
  3. Follow the 4-7-8 cadence without strain.
