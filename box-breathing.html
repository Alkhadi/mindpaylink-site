Box Breathing — Setup

Equal sides like a square: inhale → hold → exhale → hold. Default 4-4-4-4.

Quick Session

Tip: Start with 3 minutes · 5.5 BPM. Tap the orb or press Space to pause/resume.

How to Practice

  1. Sit upright or lie flat; soften shoulders and jaw.
  2. One hand on chest, one on belly (diaphragmatic cues).
  3. Inhale → Hold → Exhale → Hold with equal counts (e.g., 4-4-4-4).
