Coherent Breathing (5‑5)

Inhale for 5 seconds, exhale for 5 seconds (≈5–6 BPM). Resonant pace supports HRV stability and calm.

Instructions

  1. Sit upright or lie flat; relax the jaw and shoulders.
  2. Breathe through the nose; let the belly rise and fall.
  3. Keep the inhale and exhale even (5 seconds each).
