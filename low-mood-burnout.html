Low Mood & Burnout

Use steady, light effort breathing to reduce overload and gently build momentum.

Coherent 5-5 (5 min) Box Breathing (3 min) Explore Mood Tools

Small steps

  • One short walk outside today
  • Two minutes tidy a surface
  • Text one trusted person

Repeatables

  • 5-minute breathing after meals
  • Light stretching with slow exhales
  • Regular sleep / wake times

Get help now if

You feel unsafe, have thoughts of self-harm, or symptoms are worsening — contact emergency services or a clinician.
